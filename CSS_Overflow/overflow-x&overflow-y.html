Overflow-x and overflow-y

you can also change the overflow of content horizontally or vertically.

Overflow-x specifies what to do with the left/right edeges of the content.

overflow-y specifies what to do with the top/bottom edege of the content.

You can use the overflow property when you want to have better control of the layout. The overflow property specifies what happens if content overflows an element's box.
