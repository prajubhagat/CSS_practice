Defination of Cool?

"Cool" is a subjective term that describes something or someone that is stylish, impressive, interesting, or socially appealing in a relaxed and confident way. It's often associated with qualities like originality, trendiness, confidence, and a certain level of nonchalance. What is considered "cool" can vary greatly depending on cultural, societal, and individual preferences. It can apply to fashion, technology, art, behavior, music, and various aspects of pop culture. Keep in mind that what's considered cool can change over time and across different groups of people

What is cool?

Cool is a complex and multifaceted concept that has evolved over time to encompass various meanings and contexts.
1.Coolness in Fashion: Coolness often influences fashion trends. People who are seen as cool might set fashion trends or adopt a unique personal style that's imitated by others.
2.Coolness in Media: The media, including movies, TV shows, music, and advertising, often play a role in shaping what's considered cool. Characters and celebrities can influence trends and perceptions of coolness.
